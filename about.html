About this Project

The project aims to develop a comprehensive and proactive approach to flood management in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (KP) by utilizing various analytical tools and integrating historical flood data to identify flood-susceptible areas. The ultimate goal is to make this flood susceptibility data accessible to all stakeholders, enabling them to adopt a proactive approach to flood preparedness and response.
